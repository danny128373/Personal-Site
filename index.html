About Me

Currently attending Nashville Software School. My love for software development started 10 years ago while I was attending Middle Tennessee State University and taking a C# class for fun. Software development has been a hobby of mine ever since. I enjoy dissecting a challenging problem and splitting it into manageable pieces. I love learning programming languages and keeping up with new technologies! Also, on the weekend, working for Wells Fargo as a Lead Teller. I thoroughly enjoy working at Wells Fargo. I have developed quite a few skills such as working as a team, helping customers, and dealing with difficult situations. Before working at Wells Fargo, I was a Math teacher for grades ranging 8-12. It was awesome making a positive impact to each and every student. All while doing what I enjoy, which is to solve challenging problems (Math has a few of those...). Now more excited than ever to see where my new journey of programming takes me!
